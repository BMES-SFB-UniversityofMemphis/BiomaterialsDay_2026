Welcome to Biomaterials Day 2026

We are excited to invite undergraduate and graduate students, faculty, and industry professionals to the annual Biomaterials Day conference hosted by the University of Memphis BMES/SFB student chapter. This event brings together the biomaterials community to share cutting-edge research, network with peers, and explore the latest advances in the field.

Event Details

Date: March 20, 2026

Time: 8:30am - 5:00pm

Location: FedEx Institute of Technology

Registration: Free for everyone

What to Expect

  • Keynote presentation from SweetBio Chief Science Officer, Issac Rodriguez
  • Oral presentation sessions featuring student and faculty research
  • Poster presentations showcasing innovative projects
  • Networking opportunities with industry representatives
  • Awards and recognition for outstanding presentations

Who Should Attend

Biomaterials Day is designed for anyone interested in the intersection of materials science, engineering, and biology. Whether you're an undergraduate student exploring research opportunities, a graduate student presenting your work, or an industry professional looking to connect with emerging talent, this conference offers valuable insights and connections.

Call for Abstracts

We are accepting abstract submissions for oral and poster presentations. Share your research with the biomaterials community and compete for presentation awards. Abstract submission deadline: February 16.
